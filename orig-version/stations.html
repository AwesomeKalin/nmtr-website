History of NovaMTR Stations

The NovaMTR network began as a small collection of experimental lines and stations (namely, Spawn) built by early community members. Over time, stations evolved from simple platforms into fully realised architectural landmarks. Each expansion brought new design standards, improved accessibility, and a stronger focus on player, builder and an enjoyable experience.

Today, NovaMTR’s stations reflect the creativity and engineering talent of our builders — blending function, style, and the unique personality of every area they serve. As the network continues to grow, each new station becomes a chapter in the ongoing story of NovaMTR.
